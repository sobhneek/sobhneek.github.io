<h2 style="margin-top: 0; padding-top: 0;">Private Equity Ownership, Quality of Care and
the Financial Health of California Hospitals</h2>

From [Hahnemann Hospital](https://www.newyorker.com/magazine/2021/06/07/the-death-of-hahnemann-hospital) to 
[Steward Health Care](https://www.beckershospitalreview.com/finance/the-ongoing-steward-financial-saga-17-things-to-know.html), Private Equity (PE) firms have increasingly been placed under public scrutiny for their roles in pillaging hospitals. Simultaneously, the academic literature examining the impact of PE in healthcare has grown substantially. Over the past year, I have investigated the differences in financial health, quality, and patient experience before and after ownership transitions.”
